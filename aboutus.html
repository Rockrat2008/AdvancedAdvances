Who are we?

The idea for Adanced Advances was founded by an EP Professional who wanted a better way to create and store advances. The first process that was tried was creating Microsoft Word documents, but that was ultimately just a paper advance stored on the computer. It helped with long term storage but didn't help with any other issues. Then the advances were moved to Microsoft OneNote. This was another step in the right direction, it aided with sharing (as long as everyone had a oopy of Microsoft OneNote), but still wasn't easy to use in the field and had no real security built into it.

It is for the above reasons that Advanced Advances was born. With Advanced Advances the issues of long term, safe, storage of your advances and client information, immediate sharing and updating, and security all addressed from the perspective of an actual EP professional who is currently working in the industry with ultra-high net worth clients.
